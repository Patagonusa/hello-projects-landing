FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I qualify for government-supported renovation programs?
Eligibility depends on factors like home age, current energy efficiency, income level, and location. Our quick assessment determines your qualification status and available programs in under 2 minutes.
What types of upgrades are covered by these programs?
Eligible upgrades include roofing, solar panels, HVAC systems, insulation, energy-efficient windows, tankless water heaters, exterior paint, and landscaping improvements designed to reduce energy costs.
Do I need to pay upfront for the renovations?
Many government-supported programs offer zero upfront cost options for qualified homeowners. We'll explain all payment options during your eligibility consultation.
How long does the application and renovation process take?
The application process typically takes 2-3 weeks for approval. Renovation timelines vary by project scope but most energy efficiency upgrades are completed within 4-6 weeks of approval.
