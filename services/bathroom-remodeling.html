What's Included in Our Bathroom Remodeling

Whether you're updating a guest bath or creating a master suite retreat, we handle every detail from design to final installation with expert craftsmanship.

Walk-In Showers

Replace outdated tubs or shower stalls with modern, spacious walk-in showers. We build frameless glass enclosures, curbless entries, and custom tile designs that elevate your daily routine.

  • Frameless glass enclosures
  • Curbless zero-entry designs
  • Rain showerheads and body jets
  • Built-in niches and corner shelves
  • Linear drain installations

Tub-to-Shower Conversions

Gain space and accessibility by converting your bathtub into a beautiful walk-in shower. Our conversions are fast, clean, and designed to maximize your bathroom's footprint.

  • Full tub removal and wall prep
  • Custom shower pan installation
  • Waterproof membrane systems
  • Matching tile and glass work
  • Completed in as little as 5 days

Tile Work & Surfaces

From floor to ceiling, our tile experts install stunning surfaces that are both beautiful and built to last. We work with porcelain, ceramic, natural stone, and large-format tiles.

  • Floor-to-ceiling shower tile
  • Heated tile floor systems
  • Accent walls and feature patterns
  • Porcelain, marble, and travertine
  • Waterproof backer board installation

Vanities & Fixtures

Upgrade your vanity, faucets, and hardware for a fresh, cohesive look. We offer single and double vanity options in every style from modern floating to traditional furniture-style.

  • Single and double vanity installations
  • Floating and wall-mounted designs
  • Quartz and granite vanity tops
  • Undermount and vessel sinks
  • Modern faucets and hardware

ADA Accessible Bathrooms

We specialize in accessible bathroom designs that combine safety with style. Our ADA-compliant renovations ensure comfort and independence for all family members.

  • Grab bars and safety rails
  • Barrier-free shower entries
  • Comfort-height toilets
  • Non-slip flooring surfaces
  • Wider doorways and clearances

Luxury Upgrades & Small Bath Solutions

No bathroom is too small or too ambitious. We maximize compact spaces with clever storage and open up larger baths with spa-inspired luxury features.

  • Freestanding soaking tubs
  • Steam shower systems
  • Backlit mirrors and LED vanity lighting
  • Space-saving pocket doors
  • Recessed medicine cabinets and storage

Our 4-Step Bathroom Remodeling Process

We take the complexity out of bathroom renovations with a proven, transparent process that keeps you informed and your project on track.

1

Consultation

We evaluate your bathroom, discuss your goals, review accessibility needs, and deliver a detailed, transparent estimate at no cost to you.

2

Design

Our team creates a custom design with layout options, material samples, and a clear scope of work so there are no surprises down the road.

3

Build

Our licensed contractors handle demolition, plumbing, electrical, waterproofing, tile, and fixture installation with precision craftsmanship.

4

Walkthrough

We inspect every detail with you, test all fixtures and drains, and make sure you're completely satisfied before the project is closed.
