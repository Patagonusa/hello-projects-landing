Our Services

Complete Roofing Solutions for Los Angeles Homeowners

From new installations to emergency repairs, we provide comprehensive roofing services to protect your home. Licensed CSLB #1135440.

New Roof Installation

Complete roof installation with premium materials and expert craftsmanship. We work with tile, shingle, flat, and metal roofing systems.

  • Architectural asphalt shingles
  • Clay and concrete tile roofing
  • Standing seam metal roofs
  • TPO and modified bitumen flat roofs
  • 25-50 year manufacturer warranties

Re-Roofing & Replacement

Upgrade your aging roof with modern materials and improved protection. We handle full tear-off, deck inspection, and new installation.

  • Old roof removal and disposal
  • Roof deck inspection and repair
  • Upgraded underlayment installation
  • New flashing and ridge vents
  • Energy-efficient cool roof options

Roof Repair

Fast, reliable repairs to stop leaks and prevent further damage. Our team diagnoses the root cause and provides lasting solutions.

  • Emergency leak repair
  • Missing or damaged shingle replacement
  • Flashing repair around vents and chimneys
  • Gutter and downspout repair
  • Skylight leak repair

Leak Detection & Waterproofing

Advanced leak detection technology and professional waterproofing to keep your home dry and protected year-round.

  • Infrared moisture detection
  • Water testing and source identification
  • Waterproof membrane installation
  • Deck coating and sealing
  • Preventive maintenance programs

Storm Damage Restoration

Wind, rain, and weather damage restored quickly and professionally. We work directly with insurance companies for hassle-free claims.

  • Emergency tarping and board-up
  • Insurance claim assistance
  • Wind and hail damage repair
  • Full roof replacement if needed
  • Documentation and photo reports

Free Roof Inspection

Comprehensive roof inspection by our licensed professionals. We assess your roof's condition and provide a detailed report with recommendations.

  • Full interior and exterior inspection
  • Photo documentation of findings
  • Written condition report
  • Repair vs. replacement recommendation
  • No-obligation repair estimate
