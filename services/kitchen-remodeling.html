What's Included in Our Kitchen Remodeling

Every kitchen remodel is tailored to your lifestyle, space, and budget. We handle everything from design through final walkthrough so you can enjoy your dream kitchen without the stress.

Custom Cabinetry

We design and install cabinets that maximize storage and match your style. Choose from a wide range of finishes, hardware, and configurations.

  • Shaker-style and flat-panel designs
  • Soft-close drawers and hinges
  • Pull-out organizers and lazy Susans
  • Custom pantry and island cabinetry
  • Wood, thermofoil, and painted finishes

Premium Countertops

Select from the finest countertop materials sourced from top suppliers. We measure, fabricate, and install with precision for a flawless finish.

  • Quartz (Caesarstone, Cambria, Silestone)
  • Natural granite in 50+ colors
  • Elegant marble (Carrara, Calacatta)
  • Durable quartzite options
  • Butcher block and waterfall edges

Backsplashes & Tile Work

Add personality and protection to your kitchen walls with expertly installed backsplashes that tie the entire room together.

  • Subway tile and herringbone patterns
  • Mosaic and glass tile accents
  • Natural stone slab backsplashes
  • Full-height backsplash installations
  • Grout sealing and waterproofing

Flooring Installation

We install durable, beautiful flooring that withstands the demands of a busy kitchen while complementing your new design.

  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP) flooring
  • Porcelain and ceramic tile
  • Hardwood and engineered wood
  • Heated floor systems available
  • Waterproof and scratch-resistant options

Lighting & Electrical

Proper lighting transforms your kitchen's ambiance and functionality. We design layered lighting plans that work for cooking, dining, and entertaining.

  • Under-cabinet LED lighting
  • Recessed can lights and dimmers
  • Pendant lights over islands
  • Updated electrical outlets and USB ports
  • Smart lighting controls

Layout Optimization & Plumbing

Redesign your kitchen layout for better flow, more counter space, and improved functionality. We handle all plumbing relocation and upgrades.

  • Open-concept wall removal
  • Kitchen island additions
  • Sink and faucet upgrades
  • Garbage disposal installation
  • Gas line work for cooktops
