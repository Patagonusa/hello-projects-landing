Complete Residential Electrical Solutions

Whether your Los Angeles home needs a panel upgrade, a full rewire, or modern lighting throughout, Hello Projects Pro has the licensed electricians to get it done right. We handle every aspect of residential electrical work with a focus on safety, code compliance, and long-lasting results.

Our team is experienced with the unique demands of LA homes, from older Reseda properties that need updated wiring to modern Sherman Oaks builds requiring smart home integration. Every project is completed to National Electrical Code (NEC) and California Title 24 standards.

  • ✓
    Electrical Panel Upgrades — Upgrade from 100A to 200A service to support modern appliances, HVAC systems, and EV chargers. Essential for older Los Angeles homes.
  • ✓
    Whole-House Rewiring — Replace outdated knob-and-tube or aluminum wiring with safe, modern copper wiring. Eliminate fire hazards and bring your home up to code.
  • ✓
    Lighting Installation — Recessed lighting, pendant fixtures, under-cabinet LEDs, outdoor landscape lighting, and accent lighting to transform any room.
  • ✓
    EV Charger Installation — Level 2 (240V) home charging station installation for Tesla, Rivian, and all electric vehicles. Includes dedicated circuit and panel capacity assessment.
  • ✓
    Smart Home Solutions — Smart switches, dimmers, thermostats, whole-home automation wiring, and voice-controlled lighting systems.
  • ✓
    Ceiling Fan Installation — New ceiling fan wiring, fan-rated box installation, and replacement of existing fixtures throughout your home.
  • ✓
    Outlet & Switch Upgrades — GFCI outlets for kitchens and bathrooms, USB outlets, tamper-resistant receptacles, and dimmer switch installation.
  • ✓
    Code Compliance & Inspections — Electrical safety inspections, permit coordination, code violation corrections, and pre-sale home electrical evaluations.

⚡ Safety-First Approach

Electrical work is not a DIY project. Our licensed electricians carry proper insurance, pull all required permits, and ensure every connection meets or exceeds California electrical codes. Your family's safety is our top priority on every job.
